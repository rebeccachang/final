Drinks.

Flavored Milk; This is no ordinary milk. Koreans don't fuck around with their milk. Their milk comes in cute little plastic containers and has various flavors such as banana, melon, & strawberry. Usually comes with a cute little straw you poke through the top for quick and easy refreshment.

Why does Korean Milk taste different?

"It's the UHT. If you look at the ingredients, you'll see 130-135 ºC, 2초 (seconds). That high heat burns/caramelizes the milk sugars, which means more sweetness.  For a less sweet taste, try the low-temperature milk like Pasteur or Maeil 63º. The printing on the box/bottle will show something around 63 ºC, 30분 (minutes)."
-Snowco

Yogurt Drink; Tiny refreshing yogurt flavored drink. Classic drink that can be served both cold and frozen. Yogurt flavor is not too overpowering, which makes it a favorite drink of many.

Easy for children and adults to drink, and usually comes in packs. While this drink usually comes in only one flavor, there are many other types of products that try to use this popular flavor...

Isul Tok Tok; I have no idea why is has such a strange name. But this drink is both delicious and adorable. A type of peach alcohol, only 3%, and is a popular choice for fruity, pleasant tasting alcohol.

It is also common for alcohol to be marketed by cute/hot celebrities. This one features "IU", who is an extremely popular singer in Korea. She's basically our princess. Long live IU.

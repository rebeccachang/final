Ppushu Ppushu; Delicious ramen-like snack. Crunchy and has lots of flavor! Comes in these flavors: Grilled chicken, marinated beef, spicy rice cake, and BBQ.
Make sure to follow the directions carefully! These noodles are made to be eaten crunchy, not cooked.

Directions
1. Open bag and remove flavor packet

2. Carefully crush noodles into desired size

3. Empty flavor packet in bag of crushed noodles

4. Seal opening of bag and SHAKE

5. Open up and enjoy.
